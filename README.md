🎨 MeMeraki Art Finder – Product Growth Prototype
Overview
This project is a product growth prototype built as part of the MeMeraki Product Challenge.
The objective was to design and prototype an experience that helps first-time visitors confidently discover and purchase Indian artworks by reducing choice overload through personalized recommendations.
Instead of asking users to browse 10,000+ artworks across 300+ traditional Indian art forms, this prototype introduces an **AI-inspired Art Finder Quiz** that recommends artworks based on the user's preferences.
Problem Statement
After evaluating MeMeraki's website from the perspective of a first-time customer, I observed that the challenge is not attracting traffic but helping users make confident purchase decisions.

Users often struggle with:
- Choosing from thousands of artworks
- Understanding which artwork suits their home
- Matching art with their interior style
- Deciding whether the artwork justifies its price

This results in decision fatigue and lower conversion rates.

Proposed Solution
The prototype introduces a guided recommendation experience called Art Finder.

Quiz Flow
The recommendation engine asks users:
  1. Where will you place your artwork?
  2. What is your interior style?
  3. Which colour palette do you prefer?
  4. What is your budget?
  5. What artwork size do you prefer?
Based on these responses, the system recommends the most suitable traditional Indian art forms and curated artworks.
